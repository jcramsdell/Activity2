About Me

I tried playing a wide multitude of video games on it, ranging from Skyrim to Diablo 3. All of which where graphically intense. I taught myself how to navigate effectively in computers through the modding of Oblivion and Skyrim. Constantly accessing game files to make mods work helped me understand where files in the laptop where located. Over time this usage of my laptop as a gaming laptop caused it to slow down. The system just couldn't handle anymore of my attempts to play video games are largely downscaled resolutions and poor graphics. I am sure the files in my first laptop where messed up because of how often I was editing game and computer files. My dad bought me a second laptop my Junior year and I was smart enough not to mess it up like my last laptop. But I still wanted to play video games on pc and so I started saving my money to build my own desktop computer as of my Junior year in highschool. During the time I was saving up money and working I also was researching all the necessary parts. Education about computer components became very important because I had to assemble the desktop myself and buy the correct components. This was a lot of fun and taught me the basics of building a computer while also giving me a tool that I am able to utilize for education and entertainment purposes.
